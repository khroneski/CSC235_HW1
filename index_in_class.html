Old World Swallowtail



Submitted by user smoothj282. Papilio machaon, the old world swallowtail, is a species of swallowtail that naturally occurs in Europe, Africa, Asia and even North America. It prefers to lay eggs on Apiaceae plants. It is a medium sized swallowtail that is relatively easy to read and breed in captivity.
